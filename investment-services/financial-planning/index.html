FAQs

We break down the answers to our members top questions, like what a financial advisor does, what it costs and how to know if youre ready for their help.

Is a financial advisor right for me?

Once youve saved for emergencies and are on a solid financial footing, its a great idea to begin considering how youll fund your future and protect your assets.

Will you talk to me about other investment opportunities, too?

Choosing the right mix of investments requires an in-depth understanding of your investment objectives, risk tolerance and how long you have to save. Well work with you to develop a personalized plan that best fits your goals and finances.

I need more help, but Im not ready to speak to a financial advisor yet. What should I do?

Whether youre just beginning to save for a rainy day or want to begin planning for retirement, we have educational resources to help you build your financial confidence. When youre ready, our financial advisors will be there.

How much does it cost?

Your initial consultation is completely free. After that, any costs would depend on the kinds of services you use and the complexity of your portfolio.
